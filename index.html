Celeste Horgan, Writer.

Hi! I'm Celeste; a writer by nature and designer by training. I love distilling large-scale, technically complex problems into simple, understandable language and interfaces.

I've written developer API documentation for BlackBerry, designed and delivered training/onboarding for large colleges, and designed websites with thousands of content nodes and hundreds of thousands of viewers daily.

In my spare time, I go on walks in the mountains while listening to copious amounts of Ben Howard, I play flute with a concert band that's been rolling since the 1920's, and sometimes I even smile (but mostly I don't, it ruins my lipstick.)
